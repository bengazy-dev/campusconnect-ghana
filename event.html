About

A national scholarship for women pursuing degrees in engineering, computer science, or related fields. Includes tuition support, a monthly stipend, and a year-long mentorship with industry partners.

Who should apply

  • Women enrolled in accredited Ghanaian tertiary institutions
  • Year 2–4 undergraduate or postgraduate in STEM programmes
  • Minimum GPA equivalent 3.0 / Second Class Upper

Format & location

  • Remote information session: 8 Apr, 6pm GMT
  • In-person interview shortlist: Accra & Kumasi hubs
